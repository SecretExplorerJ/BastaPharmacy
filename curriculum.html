Curriculum Overview

Explore the subjects and coursework involved in the Pharmacy Program.

1st Year, 1st Semester

  • Pharmaceutical Calculations and Techniques
  • Perspective in Pharmacy
  • Pharmaceutical Botany with Taxonomy
  • Pharmaceutical Inorganic Chemistry (with Quantitative Analysis)
  • General Education Courses:
    • Art Appreciation
    • The Contemporary World
    • Physical Activities Towards Health and Fitness 1 (PATHFIT 1)
    • English Enhancement Program 1
    • God's Saving Actions in History, Our Story: From Abraham to Jesus
    • National Service Training Program 1

1st Year, 2nd Semester

  • Pharmaceutical Organic Chemistry
  • Introduction to Pharmacy Administration Management and Leadership
  • Human Anatomy and Physiology with Pathophysiology
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis 1 (Qualitative Pharmaceutical Chemistry)
  • General Education Courses:
    • Understanding the Self
    • The Life and Works of Rizal
    • English Enhancement Program 2
    • PATHFIT 2 (Exercise-Based Fitness Activities)
    • God in the Life of the Church, In the Sacraments and Beyond
    • National Service Training Program 2

2nd Year, 1st Semester

  • Pharmaceutical Dosage Forms, Drug Delivery Systems, and Medical Policies
  • Dispensing I (Reading and Interpreting Prescriptions)
  • Pharmaceutical Biochemistry
  • Pharmaceutical Microbiology with Parasitology
  • Physical Pharmacy
  • General Education Courses:
    • Science, Technology, and Society
    • Ethics
    • PATHFIT 3 (Self-Defense)
    • Christian Living

3rd Year, 1st Semester

  • Biopharmaceutics and Pharmacokinetics
  • Dispensing II (Medication Related Problems, Medication Safety and Medication Counseling)
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Drug Discovery, Design and Development
  • Pharmaceutical Manufacturing (with Regulatory Pharmacy, Quality Assurance and CGMP)
  • Pharmacology 2
    • Pharmacy Research Methods (with Pharmaceutical Statistics)
    • Clinical Pharmacy and Pharmacotherapeutics
    • General Education Courses:
    • Transformative Education through Value Integration

    3rd Year, 2nd Semester

  • Cosmetic Product Development (Cosmetic Product Development, Regulation and Safety Assessment)
  • Clinical Toxicology
  • Public Health Pharmacy (with Pharmacoepidemiology)
  • Health Technology Assessment & health Policy (with pharmacoeconomics)
  • Social and Administrative Pharmacy
  • Clinical Pharmacy and Pharmacotherapeutics 2
    • Pharmaceutical Marketing and Entrepreneurship
    • Legal Pharmacy and Ethics
    • Pharmacy Research and Thesis Writing

      • Transformative Education through Value Integration

    • Technical Writing

    4th Year, Internship/Practicum

    • Experiential Pharmacy Practice in Institutional Pharmacy (120 hrs)
    • Experiential Pharmacy Practice in Public Health and Regulatory Pharmacy (180 hrs)
    • Experiential Pharmacy Practice in Community Pharmacy (300 hrs)
    • Competency Appraisal 1
    • Experiential Pharmacy Practice in Hospital Pharmacy (300 hrs)
    • Experiential Pharmacy Practice in Industrial Pharmacy (300 hrs)
    • Competency Appraisal 2
