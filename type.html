About the BS Pharmacy Program

The Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy (BS Pharmacy) is a four-year undergraduate degree designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies required for various pharmacy-related careers.

The program integrates pharmaceutical sciences, clinical practice, research, and technology to ensure graduates are well-prepared for roles in community and hospital pharmacies, the pharmaceutical industry, regulatory agencies, and academia.

Program Structure

1. General Education Courses

  • Purposive Communication
  • Understanding the Self
  • Mathematics in the Modern World
  • The Contemporary World
  • Science, Technology, and Society

2. Professional Courses

  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry – Study of drug composition, synthesis, and analysis.
  • Pharmacognosy – Exploration of medicinal drugs derived from natural sources.
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology – Understanding drug effects on the body and toxicological principles.
  • Pharmaceutics – Focus on drug formulation, compounding, and manufacturing.
  • Clinical Pharmacy & Pharmacotherapeutics – Application of drug therapy in patient care.

3. Experiential Learning

  • Internships – Supervised rotations in community, hospital, and industrial pharmacy settings.
  • Research & Thesis Work – Conducting studies to contribute to pharmaceutical advancements.

Integration of Informatics in Pharmacy Education

The BS Pharmacy curriculum in the Philippines now includes pharmacy informatics, ensuring graduates are proficient in using health information systems, electronic medical records (EMRs), and medication management technologies.

This integration prepares pharmacy students for roles in clinical decision support, telepharmacy, and digital healthcare innovation.
